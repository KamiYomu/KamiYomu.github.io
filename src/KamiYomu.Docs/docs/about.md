As a manga enthusiast and developer, I found myself frustrated with the volatile nature of online reading platforms. Sites would disappear, libraries would be censored, and tracking cookies followed every page turn. I wanted something different.

## How it Works

KamiYomu acts as a bridge between the vast world of web-based manga and your private home server.



1.  **Crawl:** Use specialized agents to safely fetch content.
2.  **Archive:** Store high-quality images in a structured, local format.
3.  **Read:** Access your library through a beautiful, responsive web interface hosted by you.
